# pokemon-data-analysis
Analysis on a Pokemon Data set containing information like name, type, total score, hp, attack, defense, etc.

## Questions 
1. What is the relationship between a Pokémon's generation and total stats? Are newer generations stronger than older ones, or has the game stayed balanced over time?
2. Are Pokémon with the highest total stats always the ones with the highest attack stat? Or do Pokémon tend to be balanced across all stats?
3. Are legendary Pokémon actually superior to non-legendary Pokémon across all stats, or do they only dominate in certain areas? Where exactly does the "legendary advantage" show up?

## Findings
Found that generations 1-6 are pretty balanced against each other, allowing us to use just one generation as our sample population. Pokemon with the highest attack stat tend to have a higher stats overall. There is a difference between the average total stat of a legendary Pokemon and non-legendary Pokemon, however the differences are not always vast.

## Data Source
[Kaggle - Pokemon Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/abcsds/pokemon)
